Legal & Support

PRIVACY & DATA

We respect your privacy. Here's everything you need to know about how we handle your data.

PRIVACY POLICY

Blast Ship collects only the data required to run the game — your account information, game progress, and in-app purchases. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is stored securely using Firebase (Google) infrastructure. For the full Privacy Policy, please visit: sapapp-studio.site/privacy.

DATA DELETION

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, send an email to support@sapapp-studio.site with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your in-game username. Requests are processed within 30 days.
