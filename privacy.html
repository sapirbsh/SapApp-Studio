Introduction

Welcome to Blast Ship. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile game ("Blast Ship"). By playing the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2

Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Game, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you log in using Google Sign-In, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture to create your in-game profile.
  • Game Data: We store your game progress, fleet setups, spin tickets, gems, and match history on our secure cloud servers (Firebase).
  • Device & Usage Information: We automatically collect certain information such as your device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers to ensure the game functions correctly and to prevent cheating (using Firebase App Check).
3

Third-Party Services

The Game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication & Cloud Firestore (for saving game data)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (for understanding how players interact with the game)
  • Google AdMob (for displaying rewarded video ads)
4

Children’s Privacy

Our Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

5

Data Security

We value your trust in providing your personal information, and we use commercially acceptable means to protect it (such as encrypted databases via Firebase). However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@sapapp-studio.site

7

Data Deletion Policy

If you wish to delete your account and all associated game data from our servers, you do not need to have the app installed.

To request deletion:
Simply send an email to support@sapapp-studio.site with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your in-game name or the Google email you used to sign in. We will delete all your data within 7 business days.
